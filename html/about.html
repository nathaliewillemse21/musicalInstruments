Ignite Your Passion for Music with Nathalie's Musical Instruments

Nathalie's Musical Instruments is a family-run and owned store, unlike most other stores that are corporate. The business was started by the owner, Nathalie, who had a passion for music and wanted to share it with the world. She then realized that not everyone could afford musical instruments, which made her determined to do something about it.

Most of the instruments were donated, and the rest were bought out of her own pocket. We not only sell musical instruments but also have a program that allows us to give you lessons based on whatever instrument tickles your fancy.

We at Nathalie's Musical Instruments are very passionate about bringing music into people's hearts because, as they say, MUSIC HEALS THE HEART OF WOUNDS THAT CANNOT BE SEEN.

We supply all your musical needs, from drums and keyboards to guitars and violins. Visit us on Facebook or follow us on Instagram & Twitter.
